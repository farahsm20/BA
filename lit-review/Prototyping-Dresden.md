## Key Takeaways 

 - Query optimization has three components: plan enumerator, cost model, and cardinality estimator (most research focuses on improving one of these three components)
 - Problem: prototypes are often integrated directly into a specific PG version, making them hard to compare and reproduce ->
PostBOUND solves this by enabling reproducible prototyping of query optimization concepts in PostgreSQL
